Step 1 — Macroeconomic analysis


Before analyzing any company, understand the environment it operates in. Ask:

1 -Is the economy growing or contracting (GDP trend)?
2 -Are interest rates rising? (Hurts borrowers and growth stocks; helps banks)
3 -What is inflation doing? (Erodes margins if costs rise faster than prices)
4 -What is the currency environment? (Matters for exporters/importers)
5 -Are we in an early, mid, or late business cycle? (Different sectors outperform at different stages)
