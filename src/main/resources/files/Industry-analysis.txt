Step 2 — Industry analysis

Use Porter's Five Forces to assess how attractive an industry is:

1 -Threat of new entrants — low barriers = more competition = lower profits
2 -Bargaining power of suppliers — fewer suppliers = they control prices
3 -Bargaining power of buyers — price-sensitive buyers compress margins
4 -Threat of substitutes — alternatives limit pricing power
5 -Industry rivalry — intense competition destroys value

Also look at: market size and growth rate, regulatory environment, and where the industry is in its lifecycle (emerging, growth, mature, declining).
