Step 3 — Company qualitative analysis (the moat)
This is what Warren Buffett calls the economic moat — the durable competitive advantages that protect profits:

Brand power (Apple, Coca-Cola can charge premiums)
Switching costs (Salesforce customers don't leave easily)
Network effects (Visa is more valuable with more users)
Cost advantages (Walmart's scale, Amazon's logistics)
Regulatory licenses (banks, utilities, broadcasters)
Management quality — track record, capital allocation, skin in the game, integrity
